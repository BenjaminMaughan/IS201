Iron Maiden Trivia

Heavy Metal is my favorite genre of Music. Here is the history of how I became interested in Heavy Metal and my favorite things about it.


History:

When I was 14 years old, my dad gave me his MP3 player because I wanted one. At the time I didn't really have a taste in music so it didn't make much sense why I wanted one. I just thought that it would be cool. So I started listening to some of my dad's music and I came across this one band that I really liked. They were called Iron Maiden. From there, I became obsessed and listened to all of their songs. My taste in heavy metal music only grew until it became what it is today.
